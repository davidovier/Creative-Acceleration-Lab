### 3. AI-Assisted Tattoo Design

#### Vision:
Use AI + archetype mapping + symbolic extraction to create deeply meaningful tattoo concepts.

#### Service Flow:

1. **Emotional Intake**
   - Story extraction session
   - Archetype identification
   - Wound and transformation mapping

2. **Symbol Generation**
   - AI-assisted symbol exploration
   - Metaphor to visual conversion
   - Multiple design variations

3. **Refinement with Human Artist**
   - Collaborate with tattoo artist
   - Technical feasibility
   - Artistic enhancement

4. **Meaning Documentation**
   - Story behind the symbol
   - Personal significance guide
   - Evolution over time

#### Revenue Model:
- Premium design service
- Partnership with tattoo artists (revenue share)
- Symbol library licensing
- Digital design packages

#### Unique Value:
- Not just aesthetic tattoos, but meaningful identity markers
- AI speeds exploration, human ensures soul
- Connects to larger identity work
