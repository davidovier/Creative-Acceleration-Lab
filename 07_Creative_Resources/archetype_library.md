# Archetype Library

## Core Archetypes

### 1. Creator
**Essence:** Brings new things into existence

**Characteristics:**
- Innovative and imaginative
- Values self-expression
- Driven by vision
- Fears mediocrity

**Symbol:** Open circle
**Applications:**
- Brand voice: Innovative, artistic
- User flows: Exploratory, creative freedom
- Tattoo designs: Creation symbols, tools, hands

**Prompts:**
- "What beauty wants to emerge through you?"
- "What would you create if failure was impossible?"

---

### 2. Magician
**Essence:** Transforms reality through knowledge

**Characteristics:**
- Seeks understanding of universal laws
- Transformative mindset
- Visionary and mystical
- Fears unintended consequences

**Symbol:** Triangle
**Applications:**
- Brand voice: Mysterious, transformative
- Product design: Transformation-focused features
- Tattoo designs: Alchemical symbols, transformation imagery

**Prompts:**
- "What transformation are you resisting?"
- "What reality do you want to reshape?"

---

### 3. Warrior
**Essence:** Fights for what matters

**Characteristics:**
- Courageous and disciplined
- Values strength and competence
- Goal-oriented
- Fears weakness

**Symbol:** Sharp angles
**Applications:**
- Brand voice: Strong, decisive
- User flows: Direct, achievement-focused
- Tattoo designs: Weapons, shields, armor

**Prompts:**
- "What battle are you pretending you're not fighting?"
- "What are you willing to fight for?"

---

### 4. Sage
**Essence:** Seeks truth and wisdom

**Characteristics:**
- Analytical and thoughtful
- Values knowledge
- Contemplative
- Fears ignorance

**Applications:**
- Brand voice: Wise, informative
- Product design: Learning-focused, educational
- Tattoo designs: Books, eyes, light

---

### 5. Explorer
**Essence:** Discovers new territories

**Characteristics:**
- Adventurous and independent
- Values freedom
- Curious and brave
- Fears being trapped

**Applications:**
- Brand voice: Adventurous, pioneering
- User flows: Discovery-oriented
- Tattoo designs: Compasses, maps, horizons

---

### 6. Lover
**Essence:** Creates intimacy and connection

**Characteristics:**
- Passionate and devoted
- Values relationships and beauty
- Sensual and appreciative
- Fears being alone

**Applications:**
- Brand voice: Intimate, emotional
- Product design: Connection-focused
- Tattoo designs: Hearts, roses, intertwined elements

---

### 7. Rebel
**Essence:** Breaks rules and disrupts

**Characteristics:**
- Revolutionary and outrageous
- Values liberation
- Disruptive
- Fears powerlessness

**Applications:**
- Brand voice: Bold, challenging
- Product design: Anti-establishment
- Tattoo designs: Broken chains, flames, disruption symbols

**Prompts:**
- "What rule are you ready to break?"
- "What system needs disrupting?"

---

### 8. Caregiver
**Essence:** Nurtures and protects

**Characteristics:**
- Compassionate and generous
- Values service
- Selfless
- Fears selfishness

**Applications:**
- Brand voice: Nurturing, supportive
- Product design: Helper-focused, supportive features
- Tattoo designs: Hands, shields, embrace imagery

---

## Archetype Application Guide

### Use Archetypes To:
- Guide product voice and tone
- Define user flows and experiences
- Inspire tattoo and symbol designs
- Shape founder narratives
- Define brand aesthetics
- Create character-driven marketing

### Archetype Mapping Process:
1. Conduct interview or assessment
2. Identify behavioral patterns
3. Match to primary archetype
4. Consider secondary archetype blend
5. Create identity brief
6. Apply to brand/product decisions

### Archetype → Shape Mapping:
- **Creator** → open circle
- **Magician** → triangle
- **Warrior** → sharp angles
- **Sage** → spiral
- **Explorer** → arrow or compass
- **Lover** → heart or curves
- **Rebel** → jagged or broken lines
- **Caregiver** → protective curves

### Archetype Prompt Libraries

#### Creator:
- "What beauty wants to emerge through you?"
- "What would you create if judgment didn't exist?"

#### Magician:
- "What transformation are you resisting?"
- "What reality needs reshaping?"

#### Warrior:
- "What battle are you pretending you're not fighting?"
- "What's worth fighting for?"

#### Rebel:
- "What rule are you ready to break?"
- "What needs disruption in your world?"
