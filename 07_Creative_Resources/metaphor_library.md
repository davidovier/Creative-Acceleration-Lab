# Metaphor Library

## Emotion → Metaphor Mapping

### Core Emotions:

**Shame** → collapsing tower, sinking ship, shadows

**Pride** → rising sun, crowned figure, mountain peak

**Confusion** → fog, labyrinth, tangled threads

**Joy** → expanding circle, blooming flower, sunrise

**Anger** → wildfire, storm, volcano

**Hope** → seedling, dawn, lighthouse

**Fear** → hollow cave, frozen lake, darkness

**Sadness** → rain, falling leaves, fading light

**Love** → intertwined roots, flowing river, eternal flame

**Courage** → lion, sword, stepping forward

**Grief** → ocean waves, winter, empty chair

**Wonder** → starry sky, opening door, child's eyes

**Determination** → climbing mountain, forging metal, arrow in flight

**Betrayal** → broken mirror, severed rope, eclipse

**Trust** → bridge, open hands, roots and soil

**Anxiety** → tightening spiral, gathering storm, quicksand

**Peace** → still water, gentle breeze, resting bird

**Frustration** → locked door, boulder uphill, tangled knot

**Excitement** → lightning, rapids, fireworks

**Gratitude** → harvest, full moon, warm hearth

## Transformation Metaphors

### Journey Metaphors:
- **The Hero's Quest** - leaving comfort, facing trials, returning transformed
- **Metamorphosis** - caterpillar to butterfly
- **Phoenix Rising** - death and rebirth through fire
- **The Crossing** - from one shore to another
- **Climbing the Mountain** - ascension and perspective shift
- **Diving Deep** - descending to find treasure
- **Shedding Skin** - releasing old identity
- **Forging Steel** - transformation through pressure and heat

### Growth Metaphors:
- **The Seedling** - potential becoming reality
- **The River** - constant flow and adaptation
- **The Tree** - deep roots, reaching branches
- **The Spiral** - cyclical evolution
- **The Bloom** - opening and revealing
- **The Mycelium** - hidden connection and network

### Struggle Metaphors:
- **The Storm** - chaos before calm
- **The Desert** - barrenness testing resolve
- **The Labyrinth** - confusing path to center
- **The Forge** - creation through fire
- **The Battle** - conflict and courage
- **The Crucible** - testing and refinement

## Product & Founder Metaphors

### For Startups:
- **Building a Ship** - craft and journey
- **Lighting a Fire** - spark to flame
- **Planting a Garden** - cultivation and patience
- **Forging a Weapon** - purpose and precision
- **Weaving a Tapestry** - interconnected elements

### For Corporate Transformation:
- **Breaking Chains** - liberation from constraints
- **Opening Windows** - fresh air and perspective
- **Demolition** - clearing space for new
- **Alchemy** - transforming base to gold
- **Revolution** - complete system change

### For Creative Work:
- **Channel** - conduit for expression
- **Excavation** - unearthing what's buried
- **Translation** - making invisible visible
- **Midwifery** - assisting birth of ideas
- **Mirror Making** - reflecting truth

## Narrative Metaphors

### Story Structures:
- **The Quest** - hero seeks object/truth
- **Rags to Riches** - transformation through adversity
- **The Voyage and Return** - journey to unknown and back
- **Rebirth** - resurrection after fall
- **Overcoming the Monster** - defeating external threat
- **Tragedy** - fatal flaw leads to downfall
- **Comedy** - order from chaos

### Character Metaphors:
- **The Wounded Healer** - transformation through suffering
- **The Reluctant Hero** - growth through necessity
- **The Guide** - wisdom keeper
- **The Shadow** - rejected self seeking integration
- **The Trickster** - chaos and change agent
