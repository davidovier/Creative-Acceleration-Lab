# Brand Aesthetic Guidelines

## Color Palette
- Charcoal
- Bone
- Copper
- Muted gold

## Visual Elements
- Symbols
- Line art
- Sacred geometry
- Handwritten sketches

## Tone
- Honest
- Artistic
- Anti-bureaucratic

## Brand Tone Calibration

### Voice Settings
- 30% mystical
- 30% artistic
- 20% technical
- 20% rebellious

### Forbidden Elements
- Corporate cliches
- Over-formal tone
- Unnecessary complexity

## Tattoo-Inspired UI Style Guide
- Thick line separators
- Soft gradients with earthy tones
- Symbolic corner accents
- Minimalistic, intentional spacing

## System Design Patterns Inspired by Tattoo Art
- Bold line boundaries → feature boundaries
- Shading → progressive enhancement
- Negative space → intentional simplicity
- Flow direction → UX pathing

## UX Writing Guidelines

### Tone
- Symbolic
- Minimalistic
- Honest

### Forbidden Language
- "synergy"
- "stakeholders"
- "deliverables"

### UX Patterns
- Ritual-like flows
- Soft transitions
- Meaningful microcopy

## Artistic Principles
- Every output must feel alive
- Every product must carry meaning
- Every symbol must reflect emotion
- Every design must respect intuition
