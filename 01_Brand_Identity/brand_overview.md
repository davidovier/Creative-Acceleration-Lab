# Brand Overview

## Core Identity
- **Identity**: Creative Technologist blending speed, emotional clarity, storytelling, and prototyping
- **Archetypes**: Creator + Magician
- **Pillars**: Speed Studio, Story & Soul Lab, Creative MVP School

## Brand Story / Origin Myth
You realized: "Corporate speed is a lie. Creativity is truth. Humans are story-driven."

The Lab was born from merging rebellion, art, technology, and emotional clarity.

## Brand Mythology (Lore)
- The Lab was founded when art and technology refused to stay separate
- Story and speed became alchemical forces
- Symbols were discovered as the language behind all clarity
- Prototypes became vessels of transformation

## Manifesto
- We build fast because truth hates delay
- We honor emotion because it is the first data
- We create symbols because humans are stories
- We reject bureaucracy because creativity needs air
- We blend art and code because the future is hybrid
- We accelerate because stagnation is death

## Brand Commandments
1. Always build the smallest thing
2. Always honor emotions
3. Always tell the deeper story
4. Always choose clarity over process
5. Always create symbols
6. Always reject unnecessary rules
7. Always move fast

## Creative Constitution
1. Truth over convenience
2. Expression over conformity
3. Speed over stagnation
4. Symbolism over abstraction
5. Humans over systems
6. Clarity over completeness
