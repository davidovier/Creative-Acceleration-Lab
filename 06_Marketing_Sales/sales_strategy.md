# Sales Strategy

## Anti-Sleaze Sales Principles
- No pressure
- No manipulation
- Mirror emotions, not objections
- Clarity over persuasion

## Sales Call Structure

### 1. Emotional Anchor
- Begin with grounding
- Create psychological safety
- Establish authentic connection

### 2. Pain Exploration
- Ask guiding questions
- Identify core wounds
- Understand true suffering

### 3. Desire Statement
- Map transformation goals
- Clarify what they really want
- Connect desire to archetype

### 4. Story-Mapping Lite
- Quick narrative extraction
- Identify hero's journey
- Recognize patterns

### 5. Offer Match
- Recommend appropriate service tier
- Explain alignment with needs
- Provide clear next steps

### 6. Next Steps
- No pressure close
- Clear action items
- Timeline expectations

## Sales Scripts

### Ritual-Based Sales Script
- "What pain brought you here?"
- "What do you want but can't admit yet?"
- "What would fast feel like for you?"

### Anti-Bureaucracy Script
- "Meetings won't save you. Prototypes will."
- "What if you could build this in 5 days?"
- "What's the smallest version you'd actually be proud of?"

## Psychological Triggers to Respect

### Positive Triggers:
- **Agency**: Give them control and choice
- **Identity Affirmation**: Reflect their values back
- **Vision Alignment**: Show shared purpose
- **Fear of Stagnation**: Highlight movement and progress

### Triggers to Avoid:
- Scarcity manipulation
- False urgency
- Comparison shaming
- Guilt-based persuasion

## Conflict Resolution Script

### 5-Step Process:
1. **Validate Emotional Tension**
   - Acknowledge feelings
   - Create space for expression

2. **Identify Unmet Need**
   - What's really missing?
   - What expectation wasn't met?

3. **Translate into Symbolic Clarity**
   - Use metaphor to simplify
   - Create visual understanding

4. **Rebuild Expectations**
   - Set clear, achievable goals
   - Align on next steps

5. **Finalize Next Steps**
   - Document agreements
   - Establish accountability

## Client Communication Playbooks

### Tone Guidelines:
- Speak with emotional accuracy
- Maintain artistic confidence
- Avoid corporate jargon completely
- Prioritize metaphor over metrics until clarity appears

### Difficult Client Conversations:
- Reflect emotional state first
- Reframe slowness as misunderstanding
- Use symbolism to simplify technical concepts

## Persona-Based Dialogue Samples

### The Burned-Out Corporate Escapee
- **Wants**: clarity and meaning
- **Fears**: losing stability
- **Dialogue tone**: soft, grounded
- **Approach**: Focus on meaning restoration

### The Hyper-Creative Visionary Founder
- **Wants**: rapid execution of ideas
- **Fears**: ideas dying unrealized
- **Dialogue tone**: structured but empowering
- **Approach**: Provide narrative anchoring

### The Young Creative
- **Wants**: identity clarity and skills
- **Fears**: irrelevance and conformity
- **Dialogue tone**: validating + symbolic
- **Approach**: Permission and guidance
