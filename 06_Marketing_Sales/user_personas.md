# User Personas

## Primary Personas

### 1. The Young Creative
**Demographics:**
- Age: 22-30
- Background: Design, arts, or creative fields
- Career stage: Early or transitioning

**Psychographics:**
- Lost and identity-seeking
- Expressive and emotionally open
- Craves permission to create
- Fears conformity and irrelevance

**Needs:**
- Clarity + permission
- Skills development
- Identity exploration
- Community belonging

**Services Match:**
- Creative MVP School
- Creative Identity Compass
- Community membership

**Communication Style:**
- Validating + symbolic
- Encouraging and empowering
- Visual and metaphorical

---

### 2. The Visionary Founder
**Demographics:**
- Age: 28-45
- Background: Entrepreneurship, tech, startups
- Career stage: Building or scaling

**Psychographics:**
- Fast mind, blocked execution
- Frustrated by slowness
- High ambition, low patience
- Story-driven but needs structure

**Needs:**
- Story + prototype alignment
- Rapid execution
- Narrative clarity
- Technical acceleration

**Services Match:**
- 5-Day Prototype Ritual
- AI Feature Sprint
- Human Story Engine

**Communication Style:**
- Structured but empowering
- Fast-paced and action-oriented
- Results-focused with emotional depth

---

### 3. The Corporate Escapee
**Demographics:**
- Age: 35-50
- Background: Corporate roles, management
- Career stage: Transitioning or reinventing

**Psychographics:**
- Burned-out and seeking meaning
- Spiritually inclined
- Values autonomy
- Fears losing stability

**Needs:**
- Meaning restoration
- Identity reconnection
- Safe transition path
- Creative expression outlet

**Services Match:**
- Story & Soul Lab
- Archetype Mapping
- Creative Identity Compass

**Communication Style:**
- Soft and grounded
- Deep and meaningful
- Patient and supportive

---

## Emotional Diagnostics by Persona

### Founder Emotional States

#### The Visionary
- **State**: Full of ideas, needs structure
- **Intervention**: Narrative anchoring + prototype discipline

#### The Burned-Out
- **State**: Lost meaning, needs purpose
- **Intervention**: Story extraction + symbolic reconnection

#### The Overthinker
- **State**: Analysis paralysis, needs momentum
- **Intervention**: Speed discipline + MVP methodology

#### The Artist
- **State**: Self-doubt, needs permission
- **Intervention**: Identity affirmation + creative rituals

#### The Skeptic
- **State**: Trust issues, needs proof
- **Intervention**: Case studies + incremental validation

---

### Team Emotional States

#### Fear-Based Stagnation
- Paralyzed by risk aversion
- Needs: Psychological safety + small wins

#### Bureaucratic Armor
- Hidden behind process
- Needs: Permission to simplify + leadership clarity

#### Misaligned Incentives
- Working at cross-purposes
- Needs: Emotional honesty + vision alignment

#### Creative Starvation
- Talent underutilized
- Needs: Expression opportunities + autonomy
