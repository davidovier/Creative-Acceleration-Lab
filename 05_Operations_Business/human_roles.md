# Human Role Job Descriptions

## Core Team Roles

### 1. Creative Technologist (Founder)
**Responsibilities:**
- Vision holding
- System creation
- Emotional signaling
- Artistic direction
- Prototype supervision

**Key Skills:**
- Technical expertise
- Emotional intelligence
- Narrative design
- Strategic thinking

### 2. Ritual Facilitator
**Responsibilities:**
- Guides emotional sessions
- Uses symbolic frameworks
- Ensures psychological safety

**Key Skills:**
- Coaching/therapy background
- Deep empathy
- Archetype knowledge
- Trauma-informed practices

### 3. Prototype Assistant
**Responsibilities:**
- Builds supporting features
- Creates UX sketches
- Maintains speed discipline

**Key Skills:**
- Rapid prototyping
- UX/UI design
- Low-code platforms
- AI integration

## Leadership Principles

### Creator Leadership
- Expression over perfection
- Vision over process
- Truth over politics
- Humanity over hierarchy

### Anti-Corporate Management
- No pointless approvals
- Transparent decision logs
- Rapid cycles
- Emotional honesty

## Future Expansion Roles

### Community Manager
- Facilitates tribe connections
- Manages online platforms
- Coordinates events

### Content Creator
- Produces educational material
- Documents case studies
- Creates marketing assets

### Technical Architect
- Designs scalable systems
- Oversees AI infrastructure
- Maintains technical quality
