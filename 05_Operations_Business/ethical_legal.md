## AI Ethics

### Transparency Requirements:
- Always disclose AI usage
- Explain how AI influences decisions
- Provide human oversight options
- Document AI decision-making

### Ethical Guidelines:
- Transparency required
- No manipulative persuasion
- Always offer human fallback
- Respect user autonomy

## Emotional Crisis Protocols

### Signs of Overwhelm:
- Repetition loops
- Identity dissonance
- Emotional collapse
- Extreme distress signals

### Response Protocol:
1. **Grounding Breath**
   - Pause the session
   - Guide breathing exercises
   - Create safety

2. **Symbolic Anchoring**
   - Use visual/symbolic grounding
   - Reference stable elements
   - Provide orientation

3. **Stabilizing Narrative**
   - Reframe experience
   - Provide context
   - Offer professional referral if needed

### Escalation Path:
- Recognize when professional help is needed
- Have referral network ready
- Never attempt to replace licensed therapy
- Know limitations of coaching vs. therapy

## Professional Boundaries

### Scope of Work:
- Creative coaching and mentorship
- Product development consulting
- Identity exploration through archetypes
- NOT licensed therapy or medical advice

### Client Relationships:
- Maintain professional boundaries
- Clear contracts and expectations
- Confidentiality agreements
- Transparent communication

## Data Privacy

### Data Handling:
- Secure storage of client information
- Clear data retention policies
- GDPR/privacy law compliance
- Client control over their data

### AI Data Usage:
- Transparency about data used for training
- Opt-in for sensitive information
- Anonymization when appropriate
- Clear data deletion procedures
