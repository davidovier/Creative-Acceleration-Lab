# Pricing Strategy

## Pricing Philosophy
- Value-based pricing > hourly
- Emotional clarity sessions priced like therapy or coaching
- Prototyping sprints priced like design agencies

## Pricing Calculator Logic

### Inputs:
- Complexity of project
- Emotional depth required
- AI features included
- Timeline constraints

### Outputs:
- Recommended price tier
- Service package suggestion
- Value justification

## Pricing Strategy by Tier

### Tier 1: Speed Services
**Emotional depth = multiplier**
- Base pricing for technical delivery
- Premium for emotional alignment work
- Rush fees for accelerated timelines

### Tier 2: Depth Services
**Story work = mid-tier**
- Coaching/therapy comparable rates
- Session-based or package pricing
- Includes follow-up support

### Tier 3: Scalable Products
**Community = recurring revenue**
- Subscription models
- One-time course fees
- Template marketplaces
- Tiered membership levels

## Revenue Streams

### Primary Revenue:
1. **Services** (Rituals + Sprints)
   - Custom client work
   - High-touch delivery
   - Premium pricing

2. **Courses**
   - Self-paced learning
   - Cohort-based programs
   - Certification tracks

3. **Templates**
   - UX templates
   - Narrative frameworks
   - Symbol kits

4. **Community Memberships**
   - Monthly/annual subscriptions
   - Tiered access levels
   - Exclusive resources

5. **Symbol Packs**
   - Design assets
   - Tattoo-inspired elements
   - Custom symbolism

6. **AI Tools**
   - SaaS products
   - Agent access
   - Automation tools
