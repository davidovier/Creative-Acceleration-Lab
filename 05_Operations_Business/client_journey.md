# Client Journey Overview

## Client Journey Stages

### 1. Awareness
- Sees content or prototype demo
- Discovers through social media, referrals, or content marketing
- First exposure to brand and philosophy

### 2. Curiosity
- Downloads example blueprint
- Explores website and offerings
- Engages with content and resources

### 3. Clarity Call
- Emotional + technical extraction
- Discovery session
- Archetype and need identification

### 4. Engagement
- Selects service tier
- Signs contract
- Onboarding begins

### 5. Delivery
- Sprint, Ritual, or Lab execution
- Regular check-ins
- Iterative refinement

### 6. Expansion
- Follow-up services or school enrollment
- Ongoing relationship
- Advanced offerings

### 7. Evangelism
- Becomes a movement ambassador
- Refers others
- Shares transformation story

## Service Tiers

### Tier 1: Speed Services
- 5-Day Prototype Ritual
- AI Feature Sprint
- Acceleration Audit

### Tier 2: Depth Services
- Human Story Engine
- Archetype Mapping
- Symbolic Identity Design

### Tier 3: Scalable Products
- Courses
- Community
- Templates

## Automated Client Lifecycle Workflows

### Lead → User
- Downloads symbolic guide → tags assigned → nurturing sequence

### User → Client
- Takes clarity quiz → mapped archetype → recommended service

### Client → Community Member
- Completes ritual → gets symbol badge → invited to tribe
