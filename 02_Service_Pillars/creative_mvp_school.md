# Creative MVP School

## Focus
Teaching young creatives and professionals to build fast and express identity

## Core Services
- Courses
- Workshops
- Community
- Challenges

## Curriculum

### Modules:
1. **Identity** - Archetype Discovery & Creative Identity Compass
2. **Story** - Narrative frameworks and emotional clarity
3. **Prototyping** - Build ugly first, ship daily
4. **AI Tools** - Scaffolding and feature integration
5. **Expression Rituals** - Creative practices and emotional release
6. **Portfolio Creation** - Showcasing work and identity

## Full Module Example: "Build Ugly First"
- Students draw terrible prototypes intentionally
- Goal: release perfection attachment

## Storytelling Module
- Students rewrite their career as a myth
